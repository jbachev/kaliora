About Kaliora

Built on deep operational expertise

Kaliora Management & Consulting is a Plovdiv-based advisory and venture development firm led by a senior operations executive with over 18 years of multi-site leadership experience across retail, leisure, and entertainment sectors in Central and Eastern Europe.

Having managed large-scale teams, multi-country P&L portfolios, shopping centre operations, and complex new-site launches for one of Europe's largest cinema operators, Kaliora brings board-level perspective and hands-on execution capability to every engagement.

Our team uniquely spans operations, marketing strategy, and human development — giving clients a 360° advisory partnership that addresses business performance, brand reach, and people potential within a single, integrated offer.

What we do

Services designed to move the needle

We combine operational rigour, retail expertise, marketing intelligence, and human development to deliver outcomes that are measurable, sustainable, and built for long-term scale.

Operations & Management

Multi-site operational audits, P&L ownership, KPI frameworks, and structured execution roadmaps. We identify inefficiencies and build improvement plans grounded in measurable commercial outcomes.

Retail & Shopping Centre Management

End-to-end management of retail environments and shopping centres — tenant mix strategy, footfall optimisation, operational standards, and performance benchmarking across the full retail spectrum.

Negotiations & Deal Closing

Expert support in commercial negotiations, lease agreements, supplier contracts, franchise terms, and partnership deals. We help you prepare, structure, and close agreements that protect your interests and maximise long-term value.

Marketing & Digital Strategy

Consumer behaviour analysis, digital communication, e-commerce strategy, and brand positioning — with a strong focus on practical, real-world applicability for health, hospitality, and professional services companies.

Coaching & Wellbeing

Professional coaching and psychotherapeutic support for individuals and leadership teams. We help people navigate change, build resilience, and align personal wellbeing with professional performance.

Venture Development

From concept validation to equity structuring and launch. Feasibility modelling, location analysis, and investor pitch preparation — building businesses with the right commercial foundations from day one.

Leadership & Training

Interim general management, team structuring, and training programme delivery. From 4DX execution frameworks to marketing-focused workshops for hospitality, healthcare, and professional services organisations.

Financial Modelling

Robust investment analysis using IRR, NPV, EBITDA, FCF, and MOIC frameworks. We build the financial cases that underpin strategic decisions and give investors the clarity and confidence to commit.
